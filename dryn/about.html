About Me

Greetings! I'm Dryn Manabat, a passionate 20-year-old second-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student based in picturesque Poblaction Este, Sta.Cruz, Ilocos Sur. Amidst theh world of codes and algorithms, I find solace and inspiration in the serene beauty of sunrise and sunset.

Why Sunsets and Sunrises?

In the fast-paced of world of technology, the tranquility of a sunrise and the vibrancy of a sunset serve as a reminder to pause, appreciate, and find beauty in simplicity. Through my lens, I aim to share these moments of stillness and spectacle, inviting you to join me in witnessing the beauty that unfolds when the day meets the night.

Tech Meets Nature

As a second-year student in Information Technology, I bring a unique blend of technical prowess and artistic flair to my photography. The precision I apply in coding finds its parallel in meticulous framing and editing of my photgraphs. It's the intersection of these two worlds that defines my approach and sets my work apart.

Explore with Me

Join me on this visual journey through the canvas of the sky. Let's explore the interplay of light and shadow, the hues that paint the horizon, and the moments that make time stand still. Thank you for being a part of my story.
